    FAQ

    FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTION
    WHAT IS WOOWA ?

    Woowa is a Woocommerce add-on /extension which functions are sending order detail & automatic reminder notifications to your customer’s WhatsApp (WA).

    HOW DOES IT WORKS ?

    After you install the Woowa Plugin on your WordPress, then every time a customer place orders on your woocommerce website, Woowa will automatically send a message (include images/files) to customer’s WhatsApp about order details, not only that, Woowa can also send an automatic reminder message if after a few days your customer has not yet completed the payment with the message template that you set yourself.

    WHOSE NUMBER WILL BE THE SENDER ?

    Woowa consists of 2 package options, Woowa Basic will use the operator number of the Woowa admin, while the Woowa Premium package will use your custom / personal numbers with other richer features than Woowa Basic

    DOES WOOWA IS AN OFFICIAL SERVICE FROM WHATSAPP (FACEBOOK) COMPANY?

    No, we are a separated company which only automates your normal WhatsApp applications to send messages without have to typing manually by your Owner / Customer Service

    CAN I SEND MASS MESSAGES (RANDOM BROADCAST) ?

    No you’re not ! This service is not intended to do SPAM / send random messages that are not desired by the recipient (Unsolicited Message).

    IF I USE MY PERSONAL NUMBER, CAN MY NUMBER BEEN BLOCKED BY WHATSAPP COMPANY?

    As a WhatsApp normal rule for sending WhatsApp messages to people who have not saved your contact yet, then the risk is still exist if in a sufficient number of customers either intentionally / accidentally click ‘spam’ or ‘report’ in the message that you send, even though the possibilities are very small especially if you use WhatsApp for Business,

    This risk is further reduced by giving you the option to include a checklist on your store’s checkout page which asking for approval to send order details to their WhatsApp account.
